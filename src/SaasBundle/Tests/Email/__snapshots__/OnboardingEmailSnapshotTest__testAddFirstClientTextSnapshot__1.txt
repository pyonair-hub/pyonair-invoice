No client, no invoice. Let's fix that.
============================================================

Your client list is where everything starts. Add one client and you can immediately create quotes, invoices, and track every payment they owe — all in one place.

What you'll need
------------------------------------------------------------
Three fields to get started. You can fill in the rest later.

* Client name or company name
* An email address for invoices
* Billing currency

Add your first client: http://localhost/clients/add
