Your logo. Your brand. Your invoices.
============================================================

Clients judge professionalism before they read a single line item. A branded invoice — your logo, your name — signals that you run a real business.

Two things to customize
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload your logo
   PNG, SVG, or JPG. Appears on every invoice and quote you send.

2. Set your sender name
   Customize the name clients see when your invoices land in their inbox.

Brand your invoices: http://localhost/settings

P.S. on the paid plan, you can disable SolidInvoice branding in Settings — your clients will see only your name.
