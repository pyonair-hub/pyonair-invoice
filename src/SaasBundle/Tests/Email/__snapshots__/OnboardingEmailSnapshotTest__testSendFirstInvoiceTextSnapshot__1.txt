Time to send your first invoice.
============================================================

Sending a professional invoice takes under five minutes. Pick your client, add line items, set a due date — SolidInvoice handles the formatting, delivery, and tracking.

Included on every invoice, nothing extra to configure
------------------------------------------------------------
Line items, taxes, discounts, recurring schedules, and due dates — built in. Add a payment method in the next step and your client gets a 'Pay now' button too.

Create your first invoice: http://localhost/invoices/create

P.S. tip: connect a payment method before sending and your client will see a 'Pay now' button. Online invoices get paid much faster.
