Invoices don't pay themselves.
============================================================

Connect Stripe or PayPal once and every invoice you send gets a 'Pay now' button. Your client pays from any device in seconds — you're notified the moment it goes through.

Invoices with an online payment option get paid significantly faster than bank-transfer-only invoices
When paying is easy, clients pay. Make it easy.

How it works
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Go to Settings → Payment methods and connect Stripe or PayPal (30 seconds).
2. Every invoice you send automatically includes a 'Pay now' button.
3. Your client pays. You're notified immediately. Invoice status flips to Paid.

Connect a payment method: http://localhost/payments/methods

P.S. SolidInvoice adds zero fees on top of your payment provider. You pay only what Stripe or PayPal charge.
