Overdue Reminder Schedule

Automatic reminders are sent at 1, 7, and 14 days after the invoice becomes overdue. After the final (14-day) reminder, an escalation notification is sent to internal users for manual follow-up.

Escalating Tone: Reminder messages become progressively more urgent: polite at 1 day, firm at 7 days, and urgent at 14 days overdue.
How It Works: Reminders are sent daily at 9:00 AM. Each invoice receives a maximum of 4 automated reminders (1 pre-due + 3 overdue). After the final reminder, you'll receive an escalation notification to handle the invoice manually. Paid invoices will not receive reminders.
